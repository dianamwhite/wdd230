Target Audience

The Target Audience is men and women from 23 to 60 years old with high school education, some college and college graduates. Among them people who likes to be their own boss. They are constantly learning about the latest ways of promoting their business.

Personas

Jasson Tuckett

Jasson is a 30 year old men, just married, mechanic that opened his own auto shop business three years ago. He likes to be his own boss because he manages his time, exercise every day, work in his hobbies that luckily is repairing old cars. He is always looking to attend to conventions where he can find new ways to improve his business. He started with little ideas that he found in other conventions before and since then is his inspiration to go. Even though he is a successful businessman there is always competence in the cars mechanic world so he likes to up to date and the conventions are great start.

Marcela Wile

Marcela is a 25-year-old Freelance Graphic Designer who likes to be able to work from home. She is being her own boss since she graduated two years ago. She lives alone, is a minimalist person, so she like the simple things of life. She walks her dog every day; it is her way to exercise a little. She loves to read and check the new trends of design every morning at breakfast. She is always learning about trends of design and looking for new clients. She recently started working parttime in a small graphic design business in her town to learn more the other fields of design.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Jasson is trying to find new ways to attract people to his business. He is trying to think in other services that he could add. His plan is to offer good deals and discounts. He is also offering great customer service which also helps to keep his current customers happy. He is trying to find assistance in what to do because he is not good in marketing. He need help from the Chambers Commerce. They are offering to little business assistance with marketing and they have download new dates for the next conventions for businesses.

Scenario 2

Marcela start liking the office schedule, and they already offer her a permanent fulltime job. She debated for a while if she should accept because she likes her freelance job but at the same time she loves working with people in her field and being a team instead of her alone. At the end she decides to accept but they ask her to learn certain things more about the field that they are asking for her to take. She started looking in the Chamber of Commerce. They were offering educational opportunities in her field for members. This is a great opportunity.

Scenario 3

The little city where Concise, Chamber of Commerce is stablished is holding an event to connect other businesses so each owner of the community can meet. This event will help the little business and new business to know more each other and grow in marketing opportunity. Not only other business will be attending but also future costumers and people in search of job opportunities.
